# Puzzle 4 — Quantum Switchboard: Lights Out

## In-World Framing
The Beacon's quantum relay switchboard has been scrambled by the power surge.
A 5x5 grid of relay cells are in a chaotic state — some active (glowing),
some dormant (dark). The crew must work together to deactivate every cell
simultaneously to restore the relay and unlock the next system.

## Mechanics
- A 5x5 grid of cells is shown in a shared, synchronized state.
- Clicking any cell toggles that cell AND its immediate non-diagonal
  neighbors (up, down, left, right).
- The goal is to turn ALL cells dark (deactivated) simultaneously.
- Every player's click is instantly visible to all teammates.
- Uncoordinated clicking creates chaos — the team must communicate and
  establish a sequence.
- The puzzle is solved when all 25 cells are dark.

## Strategy Notes (for Oracle reference — do NOT reveal directly)
- This is the classic "Lights Out" puzzle. A known solving strategy is
  to work row by row from top to bottom, using the row below to fix
  the row above.
- The team benefits from designating one player to click while others
  observe and coordinate.
- The starting pattern is random — each session may have a different
  initial state.
