# Puzzle 1 — Hidden Object: The Crew Quarters

## In-World Framing
The ISS Beacon's crew quarters fell into disarray after the emergency alert.
Twelve critical components were scattered across the room by the chaos of
evacuation. The crew must locate every item before systems can be restored.

## Items to Find
The following 12 items are hidden in the scene:
Laser Pistol, Helmet, Data Drive, Mini Robot, Oxygen Cylinder, Hand Tool,
Communicator, Map Scroll, Bunch of Keys, Astronaut Glove, Energy Core,
General's Badge.

## Mechanics
- Players scan a detailed 2D scene image visually.
- Each found item is checked off a list on the side panel.
- Items are deliberately camouflaged — blended into the background by color,
  scale, or perspective.
- The puzzle is complete when all 12 items are checked off.

## Oracle Hint Guidance

### Tier 1 (vague, thematic)
- Reference the chaos of the evacuation and the cluttered nature of the room.
- Suggest the crew look carefully at shadows and edges.
- Do not name any specific item or location.
- Example: "The room holds many secrets in its disorder, Seeker.
  Not all that is lost is truly hidden — some merely wait to be seen."

### Tier 2 (slightly more concrete)
- Suggest players focus on a general area of the scene (e.g. the floor,
  the shelves, near the window) without naming the item.
- Ask what items the crew has already found to narrow guidance.
- Example: "What has the crew recovered so far? The lower regions of the
  quarters often conceal what the eye dismisses as debris."

### Tier 3 (mechanic guidance, no full answer)
- Remind players that items may be camouflaged by matching the background.
- Suggest changing perspective — looking for silhouettes rather than
  full shapes.
- Example: "The engineers who hid these supplies were clever. Look not for
  the object itself, but for the outline it carves against what surrounds it."

## Key Lore References
- The emergency alert was triggered by a power surge in Sector Delta-7.
- The crew evacuated in haste — items were dropped mid-task.
- The General's Badge belongs to Admiral Mustard, last seen near the helm.
