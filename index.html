Mission Briefing

How to Play

Four steps to join the mission and escape the grid.

🚀
Enter Callsign

Pick your name and crew color to identify your beacon among the stars.

🛸
Join the Ship

Teleport into the 3D spaceship shared with your crew in real-time.

🔍
Find & Claim

Move your cursor, scan the cockpit, and claim tiles to uncover hidden items.

🏆
Escape!

Coordinate with your crew to solve all puzzles and trigger the escape sequence.

Core Systems

Built for the Mission

Everything your crew needs to navigate, solve, and escape.

👥
Multiplayer

Up to 5 crew members share the same 3D space. Every cursor, every tile claim — synced in real-time via Colyseus WebSocket.

🧩
Puzzles

Navigate an 8×8 tile grid aboard a stranded spaceship. Claim sectors, coordinate strategy, and unlock the escape sequence.

🌌
3D World

Fully rendered spaceship environment powered by Three.js WebGL — no plugins, no installs, runs in your browser at 60fps.

🔮
Smart Hints

Stuck? The onboard oracle system surfaces contextual clues to guide your crew through the next obstacle.
